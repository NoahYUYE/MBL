# 5.1 指标改进预期 / Expected Indicator Improvements

## 1. 引言 / Introduction

MBL数字矿山管控塔解决方案旨在通过对Hauling运输、设备健康和能源消耗等核心环节的精细化管理，为矿山运营带来一系列积极且可衡量的改进。本文件旨在阐述方案实施后，关键绩效指标（KPI）的预期改进方向和潜在价值点。我们不提供具体的改进数字承诺，而是聚焦于描述"如何"通过系统的特定功能带来价值，以及这些价值产生的逻辑。

The MBL Digital Mine Control Tower solution aims to bring a series of positive and measurable improvements to mine operations through the fine-grained management of core aspects such as hauling, equipment health, and energy consumption. This document aims to articulate the expected improvement directions and potential value points for Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) after the solution is implemented. We do not provide specific quantitative commitments for improvement; instead, we focus on describing "how" value is generated through the system's specific functions and the logic behind this value creation.

## 2. 核心价值领域与指标改进方向 / Core Value Areas and Direction of Improvement

下表详细说明了方案在三大核心价值领域中，对关键指标的预期积极影响。

The following table details the expected positive impacts of the solution on key indicators across three core value areas.

### 2.1 运输效率 (Hauling Efficiency)

**背景**: 在单一条75公里的固定运输路线上，提升运输效率的核心在于最大化车辆的有效工作时间，减少所有非运输环节的耗时。

**Context**: On a single, fixed 75km transport route, the core of improving hauling efficiency lies in maximizing the effective working time of vehicles and minimizing time spent on all non-transportation activities.

| 核心指标 (KPI) | 指标定义 / Definition | 改进方向与价值点 / Direction of Improvement & Value Points | 关键实现功能 / Enabling Functions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **单车循环次数**<br>**Cycles per Vehicle** | 车辆在单个班次内完成"装载-运输-卸载-返回"完整流程的次数。<br>The number of times a vehicle completes the full "load-haul-dump-return" cycle within a single shift. | **1. 减少装卸排队时间**: 通过实时监控装卸点（煤场、码头）的繁忙状态和车辆的预计到达时间（ETA），智能调度车辆错峰进入，减少因排队造成的无效等待。<br>**2. 优化空车返回调度**: 卸载完成后，系统立即为车辆规划下一任务，使其以最高效的方式返回，准备下一次运输。<br><br>**1. Reduce Queuing Time at Loading/Unloading**: By monitoring the real-time status of loading/unloading points and the Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) of vehicles, the system intelligently dispatches vehicles to arrive off-peak, reducing idle time caused by queuing.<br>**2. Optimize Empty Return Scheduling**: Immediately after unloading, the system assigns the next task to the vehicle, ensuring it returns in the most efficient manner to prepare for the next haul. | - 任务规划 (Task Planning)<br>- 装载管理 (Loading Management)<br>- 驳船装载 (Barge Loading) |
| **车辆有效利用率**<br>**Vehicle Effective Utilization** | 车辆实际用于执行运输任务的时间占总工作时间的百分比。<br>The percentage of total working time that a vehicle is actually used for transportation tasks. | **1. 提升车队可用性**: 通过预防性维护，提前识别并处理潜在故障，减少车辆在运输途中的意外停机时间。<br>**2. 优化检查与交接流程**: 通过移动应用简化和规范化出车前检查与司机交接班流程，缩短准备时间。<br><br>**1. Increase Fleet Availability**: Through preventive maintenance, potential faults are identified and addressed in advance, reducing unexpected downtime during transport.<br>**2. Streamline Inspection & Handover**: The pre-departure inspection and driver shift change processes are simplified and standardized via a mobile application, shortening preparation time. | - 设备维护 (Equipment Maintenance)<br>- 车辆检查 (Vehicle Inspection)<br>- 司机管理 (Driver Management) |

### 2.2 设备健康与可靠性 (Equipment Health & Reliability)

**背景**: 设备，尤其是运输卡车，是矿山的核心资产。保障其健康和高可靠性，是保障整条运输生命线稳定的基础。

**Context**: Equipment, especially transport trucks, is a core asset of the mine. Ensuring its health and high reliability is fundamental to the stability of the entire transportation lifeline.

| 核心指标 (KPI) | 指标定义 / Definition | 改进方向与价值点 / Direction of Improvement & Value Points | 关键实现功能 / Enabling Functions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **平均无故障时间 (MTBF)**<br>**Mean Time Between Failures** | 设备从一次故障修复到下一次故障发生的平均间隔时间。<br>The average time interval between one failure repair and the next failure. | **1. 从被动维修到主动保养**: 基于设备运行数据（如发动机小时数、行驶里程），自动触发保养计划，用计划性的保养替代非计划的故障维修。<br>**2. 早期故障预警**: 实时监控关键部件参数（如温度、压力），在故障发生前识别异常趋势并发出预警，将小问题消灭在萌芽状态。<br><br>**1. From Reactive to Proactive Maintenance**: Maintenance plans are automatically triggered based on equipment operating data (e.g., engine hours, mileage), replacing unplanned failure repairs with scheduled maintenance.<br>**2. Early Fault Warning**: Real-time monitoring of key component parameters (e.g., temperature, pressure) identifies abnormal trends before a failure occurs and issues warnings, eliminating minor issues before they escalate. | - 数据监测 (Data Monitoring)<br>- 设备维护 (Equipment Maintenance) |
| **平均修复时间 (MTTR)**<br>**Mean Time To Repair** | 从发生故障到修复完成的平均时间。<br>The average time from when a failure occurs until it is repaired. | **1. 准确定位故障**: 车载诊断系统能提供精确的故障代码，帮助维修人员快速定位问题根源。<br>**2. 沉淀维修知识**: 建立电子化的维修历史和知识库，使得常见故障的解决方案可以被快速查找和复用，缩短诊断和修理时间。<br>**3. 优化备件管理**: 通过分析备件消耗，确保常用备件的合理库存，避免因等待备件而延长维修时间。<br><br>**1. Accurate Fault Diagnosis**: The onboard diagnostic system provides precise fault codes, helping technicians quickly identify the root cause of a problem.<br>**2. Accumulate Maintenance Knowledge**: An electronic maintenance history and knowledge base allow for quick retrieval and reuse of solutions for common faults, shortening diagnosis and repair times.<br>**3. Optimize Spare Parts Management**: Analysis of spare parts consumption ensures reasonable inventory levels for common parts, avoiding repair delays due to part shortages. | - 工单管理 (Work Order Mgmt)<br>- 维护记录与知识库 (Records & Knowledge Base)<br>- 备件管理 (Spare Parts Mgmt) |

### 2.3 能源效率与成本 (Energy Efficiency & Cost)

**背景**: 在混合能源（电网、柴油发电机）为电动卡车车队提供能源的模式下，通过智能化管理降低综合能源成本是核心目标。

**Context**: In a model where a hybrid energy supply (grid power, diesel generators) fuels a fleet of electric trucks, reducing overall energy costs through intelligent management is a core objective.

| 核心指标 (KPI) | 指标定义 / Definition | 改进方向与价值点 / Direction of Improvement & Value Points | 关键实现功能 / Enabling Functions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **单位公里能耗 (kWh/km)**<br>**Energy Consumption per km** | 车辆行驶单位公里所消耗的电量。<br>The amount of electricity consumed per kilometer traveled by a vehicle. | **1. 优化驾驶行为**: 通过监控和分析司机在固定路线上的驾驶习惯（如不必要的急加速/减速），提供针对性的指导和培训，培养更节能的驾驶方式。<br>**2. 轮胎健康管理**: 确保轮胎胎压处于最佳范围，减少因胎压不当造成的滚动阻力增加和电耗上升。<br><br>**1. Optimize Driving Behavior**: By monitoring and analyzing driver habits (e.g., unnecessary hard acceleration/braking) on the fixed route, targeted coaching and training can foster more energy-efficient driving practices.<br>**2. Tire Health Management**: Ensuring optimal tire pressure reduces rolling resistance and the associated increase in energy consumption. | - 驾驶行为分析 (Driving Behavior Analysis)<br>- 轮胎健康管理 (Tire Health Management) |
| **综合能源成本**<br>**Overall Energy Cost** | 为整个车队提供动力所付出的总成本。<br>The total cost incurred to power the entire fleet. | **1. 利用峰谷电价**: 系统可根据电网的峰谷电价时段，自动规划换电站的充电计划，优先在电价最低的"谷时"充电，降低充电成本。<br>**2. 优化发电机使用**: 将柴油发电机作为高成本的备用或补充能源。系统只在电网电力不足或中断时，才以最高效的模式启动发电机，避免不必要的燃料消耗。<br><br>**1. Leverage Peak-Valley Tariffs**: The system can automatically schedule the charging plan for battery swap stations based on the grid's peak and valley electricity price periods, prioritizing charging during the low-cost "valley" hours.<br>**2. Optimize Generator Usage**: Diesel generators are treated as high-cost backup or supplementary power. The system will only start the generators in the most efficient mode when grid power is insufficient or interrupted, avoiding unnecessary fuel consumption. | - 能源管理 (Energy Management)<br>- 换电站管理 (Swap Station Mgmt) |

通过在这些领域实现改进，MBL数字矿山管控塔方案将为客户带来运营效率、成本控制和资产可靠性方面的实质性价值。

By achieving improvements in these areas, the MBL Digital Mine Control Tower solution will deliver substantial value to the client in terms of operational efficiency, cost control, and asset reliability.
