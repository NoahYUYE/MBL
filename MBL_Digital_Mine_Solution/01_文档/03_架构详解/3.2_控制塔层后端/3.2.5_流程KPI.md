# 流程KPI / Process KPIs

## 概述 / Overview

流程KPI主要关注Hauling作业全流程中各环节的效率、质量和成本指标，帮助管理人员识别流程瓶颈，优化运营效率。

Process KPIs mainly focus on efficiency, quality, and cost indicators in each stage of the Hauling operation process, helping managers identify process bottlenecks and optimize operational efficiency.
